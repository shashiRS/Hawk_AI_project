About Aakruthi\xe2\x80\x99s Platina

Aakruthi\xe2\x80\x99s Platina is a residential project by Aakruthi Developers, located near Forum Value Mall, Whitefield. The project is spread across 1 acre of land and will have two blocks. This project will house around 68 units with a choice of 2BHK, 3BHK units of sizes ranging from 1100 sft to 1715 sft

Project Analysis of Aakruthi\xe2\x80\x99s Platina

    \r\n
  • Approvals status: BBMP approved
  • Demand Analysis: The demand will be high due to its proximity to several IT parks like ITPL, Salarpuria GR Tech Park and Brigade Tech Park. Whitefield is well connected with other parts of the city like KR Puram, ORR, Sarjapur Road and other localities
  • Amenities: The project will have amenities like gymnasium, swimming pool, multipurpose hall, children\xe2\x80\x99s play area, jogging track, landscape gardens, rain water harvesting, etc
  • Any Drawbacks?: The approach road is quite narrow and is in very bad condition
  • Special attractions of this project (USP):Aakruthi\xe2\x80\x99s Platina is located just 100 meters from Whitefield Main Road and its proximity to Forum Value Mall makes it a preferable location to invest in

Payment Analysis of Aakruthi\xe2\x80\x99s Platina

    \r\n
  • On Booking: Rs 100000
  • On agreement of sale (within 10 days from booking): 20% of total sale consideration (less booking amount)

Schedule for Balance payment:

After registration: 65%of total sale consideration (Completed) to be paid within 30days (as per schedule)

    \r\n
  • Completion of the foundation of the project: 10% of total sale consideration(Completed)
  • Completion of the Cellar roof slab: 10% of total sale consideration(Completed)
  • Completion of the ground floor slab: 10% of total sale consideration(Completed)
  • Completion of 1st floor slab: 5% of total sale consideration(Completed)
  • Completion of 2nd floor slab: 5% of total sale consideration(Completed)
  • Completion of 3rd floor slab: 5% of total sale consideration(Completed)
  • Completion of brickwork of the flat: 10% of total sale consideration(Completed)
  • Completion of plastering of the flat: 10% of total sale consideration(Completed)
  • Completion of flooring of the flat (except wooden flooring): 10% of total sale consideration
  • On Possession/Registration whichever is earlier: 5% of total sale consideration

Other charges:

    \r\n
  • Amenities: Rs. 100000
  • Car Parking: Rs. 200000

All the additional charges towards water charges, power charges, floor rise charges, corpus fund, maintenance charges, legal charges, stamp duty charges ,VAT & service tax etc are payable as applicable.

Note: The project has 68 units per acre, which indicates moderate density of units and moderate space
